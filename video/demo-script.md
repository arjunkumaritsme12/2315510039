# Demo Video Script

## 1. Opening the Application
- Open the local frontend at http://127.0.0.1:3000/notifications.
- Show the top-level AppBar, drawer, and navigation links.

## 2. Viewing Notifications
- Scroll through the all-notifications list.
- Highlight the notification cards, chips, and timestamps.

## 3. Filtering and Searching
- Apply a filter such as Placement.
- Type into the search bar and show the reduced set.

## 4. Pagination
- Navigate between pages and demonstrate pagination behavior.

## 5. Priority Inbox
- Open the priority page and highlight the top 10 notifications ranked by type and recency.
- Point out the score values and the highlighted cards.

## 6. Viewed vs Unviewed
- Click a card to mark it as viewed and show the gray indicator.

## 7. Responsive Layout
- Show the mobile drawer and the stacked card layout.
- Then switch to a desktop view to show the wider grid.
